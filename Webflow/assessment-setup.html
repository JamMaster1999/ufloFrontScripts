Post Assessment

Post Exam visibility is designed to provide granular control over what students can see after they take the assessment.
There are 4 categories which you can customize their permissions.
Question: The actual content of the question
Response: The student's selected response
Score: The score the student received
Correct Answer: The correct answer, set by the instructor.

Make all 4 available together

True
There are three types of visibility for each category:
1. After First Attempt: Student can see the category after they submit the first attempt and all proceeding attempts.
2. After Last Attempt: Student can see the category after their last attempt.
3. Visible on Time/Date: For manual control over when the category is visible.
*** In case you want all the categories to become available in the same way, click on the above switch.
